Features

Basic Features

  • Post statuses and short texts with "Posts", add media and details by posting under "Detailed Post", publish articles, poetry, stories by posting under the "Publication" post tab.
  • Quench your curiosity by asking questions and demonstrate your knowledge by answering others'.
  • Create groups.
  • Private messaging.
  • Follow people and businesses you love or are interested in.
  • Use "topic" tags to define and categorize your posts and content. Say goodbye to the old ways of the hashtag system! This new "topic" system provides a more organized approach for both content creators and their fans.

Special Features

  • A "Gaming" theme that transforms the bleak white background into a RGB color-shifting one!
  • An "Abyss" theme that literally darkens out the environment. Things get shady...
  • You are free to use GIFs (animated images) for your profile pictures or banners (cover pictures). This holds true for both profiles as well as groups.
  • Unlike other social platforms, Amrella's authorities do NOT track or collect private information from its users. (To be honest, we're just too lazy to do it and too liberal to let everyone just enjoy their lives.)
  • Guaranteed response if you report inappropriate content. Good for the good guys, bad for the bad guys.
  • Rated 12+, so the community is always swept clean from any 'bad' kind of content.
  • Live radio feature to provide background music as you browse.
  • Virtual currency system (similar to in-game currency) for redeeming rewards and purchasing items from the Redeem Store.
  • Special events are hosted from time to time, especially during promotions for a brand or on holidays/celebration events.
  • Very friendly developer who is always inventing and adding new features to Amrella.
